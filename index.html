LapTop

Nowadays, modern science and technology have deeply influenced every aspect of society and produced a very important impact. The development of society also with the progress of science and technology, promote the economic development of the society, so that human living standards continue to improve.

Science and technology also enrich people's experience and life. For example, TV, video and so on have reformed people's thoughts and opinions to varying degrees. However, due to people's dependence on science and technology, the development of society has produced some negative effects, which has caused a certain degree of damage to human's life and living environment.

Therefore, there is a study on laptop computers, which specifically discusses the different influences of laptop computers on students' learning in terms of opportunity, Risk, Choice and other aspects.
