Risks

With the development of network technology, the Internet onto the desk, into thousands of households, online learning, online office, online payment, online trading, online entertainment gradually become a reality. However, while people enjoy the great benefits brought by the network, they also unknowingly bear the accompanying security risks. Virus invasion, Trojan attack, malicious port scanning, strong password cracking, light will cause computer breakdown, heavy will cause data leakage and loss, causing irreparable losses or disastrous consequences. However, these security risks have not been taken seriously, especially in laptops.

Security

With the development of the Internet, computer viruses have seriously affected people's information security, property security and so on. At the present stage, the society is widely concerned about the laptop security and computer virus pre©vention work and increase the research efforts to prevent computer virus technology, in order to reduce the probability of virus invasion, improve the security and reliability of computer operation.“Security is the attribute of a computer system that ensures that it can continue to function properly after an attack”（Baecker, 2019）.

Major threats to laptop security：

There is a bug in the operating system

Even if the operating system is more advanced, more perfect, design defects are inevitable, and the existence of system design defects can lead to the laptop in danger

Virus regeneration

At the present stage, the known types of computer viruses reach more than one million, and each virus has varieties, at least tens of thousands, many hundreds of thousands of kinds, even if the timely download, install security patches, the normal use of anti-virus software, virus mutation, still may attack the laptop.

Therefore, it is necessary to install anti-virus software, anti-virus software should choose the genuine version, and in the system prompt update, upgrade, update in time, in order to give full play to the role of anti-virus software. In addition to technical preventive measures, users in the process of using a laptop, but also should form a good habit of using a laptop, as we all know, the network facilitates people's life and work, promote social progress, but with the development of the network, the emergence of viruses, a serious threat to people's computer security, One of the main reasons for the virus to invade is that people's computer use habits are not good, the lack of sufficient awareness of prevention, therefore, to promote people's awareness of protecting computer security.

Privacy

With the frequent occurrence of personal privacy leakage events at home and abroad and the importance of personal privacy protection, people are paying more and more attention to the personal privacy problems brought by computer software, mobile terminals and high technology in daily work and life. Unlike desktop computers, which are used in fixed occasions, laptops often need to accompany us in and out of various indoor and outdoor occasions, so they will face severe privacy security challenges. “Most laptops (and tablets) today come with built-in, front-facing cameras for video chat. What some users might not realize is that the cameras can be used against them for spying” (Martin, 2016).

Considering the necessity of users' actual scene operation requirements, the screen port of laptop products in the market will be equipped with cameras. The camera is usually used while the user is making a video call, but is often idle the rest of the time, which raises the question of how does the user know if the camera is working? The computer background is generally multitasking synchronous operation, most users do not know the actual performance of each running task as professional programmers. So, if in the process of the use of the product, by malicious trojans, viruses, the camera will become outlaws peep at the first window user privacy, and the consequences of its harm is immeasurable, most brands did not realize at the time of meet the demand of the application scenario, behind privacy problems of how to solve.
